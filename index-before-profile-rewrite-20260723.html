KataGomo

A multi-game AI and experimentation framework

An extension of KataGo for dozens of board games and rule variants, connecting rule implementation, search, self-play training, evaluation, and publication. Its public artifacts include selected source branches, runnable releases, neural networks, datasets, and result summaries.

KataGomo receives the largest entry today because it has the deepest public record. Future projects can take the same place as their material grows.
